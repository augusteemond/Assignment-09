How to Kickflip

Step 1 - Have a good confortable stance on your skateboard

The usual kickflip stance consists of your back foot on the tail of the skateboard ready to pop and your front foot 1 to 2 inches below the front bolts with your heel hanging off and your toe pointed.

Step 2 - Practice foot movement

In this next step, practice popping the tail of the skateboard to the ground with your back foot, while sliding your front foot twards the top of the nose of the skateboard and flick off

Step 3 - Having the skateboard kickflip under you

In this next step you will do the same process as step 2 but this time you will actually jump and have the board flip under you, rotating in kickflip motion.

Step 4 - Landing on the board

This last step is where the magic happens, once the board is flipping under you is when you will look down and spot the griptape of the board, once you see the black griptape spin twards you, you will catch the board with your back food and your front foot will follow shortly after.
